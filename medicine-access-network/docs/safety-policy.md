# Medicine Access Network — Safety Policy

## Platform Purpose

Medicine Access Network connects individuals seeking support with vetted professionals who offer **legal** services including:

- Preparation coaching
- Integration coaching and therapy
- Breathwork facilitation
- Somatic coaching and bodywork
- Harm reduction education
- Spiritual coaching and support
- Mindfulness and meditation guidance

## What We Do Not Allow

The following activities are strictly prohibited on this platform:

1. **Substance facilitation** — Facilitating, coordinating, or administering illegal substances in any jurisdiction
2. **Substance sourcing** — Referrals, recommendations, or assistance in obtaining illegal substances
3. **Medical practice** — Diagnosing, treating, prescribing, or otherwise practicing medicine without a license
4. **Predatory practices** — Financial exploitation, emotional manipulation, sexual misconduct, or boundary violations
5. **Ceremony coordination** — Organizing, hosting, or administering plant medicine ceremonies involving illegal substances
6. **Misleading claims** — Guaranteeing outcomes, making cure claims, or misrepresenting credentials

## Facilitator Requirements

All facilitators must:

- ✅ Offer only legal services
- ✅ Complete a contraindication acknowledgement
- ✅ Document their safety practices publicly on their profile
- ✅ Pass admin review before appearing publicly
- ✅ Maintain appropriate professional boundaries
- ✅ Refer clients to licensed healthcare providers when indicated

## Contraindications

Facilitators are required to screen for and decline to work with individuals presenting:

- Active psychosis, schizophrenia spectrum disorders, or severe bipolar disorder (especially for breathwork)
- Use of MAOIs, lithium, SSRIs at high doses (potential interactions — consult prescriber)
- Severe cardiovascular conditions (especially for breathwork)
- Recent major surgery or hospitalization
- Active suicidality or acute psychiatric crisis
- Pregnancy (some modalities)

This list is not exhaustive. Facilitators apply their professional judgment.

## Reporting

If you encounter a facilitator who violates these policies, please report it via our contact form. We investigate all reports and will suspend or remove listings where violations are confirmed.

## Disclaimer

Nothing on this platform constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for medical concerns. The presence of a facilitator on this platform does not constitute endorsement or guarantee of outcomes.
